Gollums tricks for slimy fish

Description

Give them to me raw and wriggling

Ingredients:

  • A small lake or river.
  • Quick hands.

Instructions:

  1. Sneaky Sneak

    2. Sneak, sneaky, precious! First, you must find yourself a nice, quiet pond or stream. Make sure to be as quiet as a mouse and as invisible as the One Ring. Gollum is the best at sneaking, yesss!

  2. Slippery Slap

    3. Find a slipper-sloppery fishy, my precious! The trick is to be quick, like a striking cobra. Plunge your skinny fingers into the water and grab the fishy right behind its gills. Be quick, or it will slip away, yes!

  3. Raw and Wriggling

    4. Hold the wiggly fishy up to your hungry lips, my precious. Feel its wiggles and squiggles in your hand. Gollum loves the wriggles, yesss!

  4. Crunchy Munch

    5. Now, don't be afraid, my precious. Take a bite, a big, juicy bite. Let the raw fishy flesh squish between your teeth. Crunch it good, just like Gollum does. Taste the river's secrets, the slimy, silvery secrets!

  5. Swiggle Down

    6. Gulp it down, my precious! Swiggle-swoggle it all the way to your belly. Feel the fishy's spirit become one with you, precious. Gollum's belly is happy now, yesss!

Remember, my precious, this is how Gollum catches and eats a fish raw and wriggling. But be careful, and don't go biting on any fishy that might be too fishy for your liking!
